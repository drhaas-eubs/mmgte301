Unit 2: The Innovator’s Dilemma

Complete these reflections before the next session. Bring your written responses — they will form the basis of our opening discussion.

Reflection 1

Which of the Five Laws of Disruption did you find most counterintuitive? Why does this law challenge conventional management wisdom, and can you identify a real company currently trapped by it?

Reflection 2

Apply the RPV Framework to an organisation you have worked for. What are its core resources, processes, and values? How do these capabilities simultaneously create disabilities that would prevent it from pursuing a disruptive opportunity?

Reflection 3

Identify one framework from the gallery that you do not yet fully understand. Return to the gallery, click into the framework, read the definition and source, and write a 200-word explanation in your own words. Bring this to the next session.
